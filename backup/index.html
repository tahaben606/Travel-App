Day-by-Day Adventure

Day 1

Arrival & First Impressions

Landed at Narita. The organized chaos of Tokyo greeted us immediately. Checked into the hotel and explored the bustling streets of Shibuya.

Day 2

Traditional Tokyo

Visited Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, took photos at the iconic red lantern, enjoyed street snacks and watched the sunset from Tokyo Tower.

Day 3

Fashion & Youth Culture

Explored Harajuku's Takeshita Street. Found amazing vintage shops and tried the trendy crepes. The energy was electrifying!

Day 4

Culinary Journey

Michelin-starred dinner in Ginza, explored hidden ramen shops, and visited the Tsukiji Outer Market. Every meal was an adventure.
